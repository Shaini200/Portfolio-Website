My Research

Automated Student Attendance Management Using Real-Time Face Recognition

Conference: Research Conference on Advances in Information and Communication Technology (RCAICT 2025)

Authors: SAS Kavindya, R Thisakaran

Published: 2025

Abstract: A facial recognition-based student attendance system was developed to automate and improve accuracy in extra classes. Using Python, OpenCV, and Tkinter, the system captures and recognizes student faces in real-time using the LBPH algorithm. Attendance is recorded automatically and exported as CSV reports. Tested with over 190 students under varying conditions, the system achieved an average accuracy of 94% with minimal errors, demonstrating its reliability and scalability for educational use.
